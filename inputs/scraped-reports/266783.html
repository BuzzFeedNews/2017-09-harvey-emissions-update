Regulated entity name VALERO CORPUS CHRISTI REFINERY EAST PLANT Physical location 1300 CANTWELL LANE
Regulated entity RN number RN100211663 City, County CORPUS CHRISTI, NUECES
Type(s) of air emissions event: EMISSIONS EVENT Event began: 09/03/2017 8:00AM
This is based on the: FINAL REPORT Event ended: 09/03/2017 12:00PM
Cause A leak was identified on the distance piece of the EP Sulfur Recovery Unit #2 (SRU2) reactor in the subsequent start up related to Hurricane Harvey. SRU2 was shut down for repairs and all acid gas was successfully routed to SRU1 without exceeding any reportable quantities. Acid Gas was contained in the Amine Unit #1 (ARU1) however the H2S concentration in the fuel gas increased resulting in SO2 emissions in the process heaters. . **** THUS, THE FINAL EMISSIONS AMOUNTS DID NOT EXCEED REPORTABLE QUANTITIES IN ANY 24 HOUR PERIOD. ****
Action taken The leak was repaired at the Sulfur Recovery Unit #2 (SRU2) thermal reactor as soon and safely as possible. After repairs were made to SRU2, it was brought back into service.
Emissions estimation method Engineering calculations were based on data from the unit from the incident.

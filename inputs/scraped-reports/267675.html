Type(s) of air emissions event: EMISSIONS EVENT Event began: 09/12/2017 5:28PM
This is based on the: INITIAL REPORT Event ended: 09/13/2017 9:46AM
Cause The AGI went down on 2nd stage scrubber level. When operations attempted to restart the compressor, it would not restart due to no oil flow on the right bank. C-1008 went down on high engine oil temperature.
Action taken The inlet rate was reduced. The scrubber was drained and the oil level was checked and was fine so operations started the AGI back up. 1008 was started up after letting the oil cool. The site's flare was maintained in constant operation during this event, flare pilots were monitored and no bypassing of the control device occurred.

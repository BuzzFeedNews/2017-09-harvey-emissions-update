Type(s) of air emissions event: EMISSIONS EVENT Event began: 09/01/2017 12:30AM
This is based on the: INITIAL REPORT Event ended: 09/01/2017 10:30PM
Cause DURING NORMAL OPERATIONS THE K-3502 REINJECTION COMPRESSOR AT THE SALT CREEK GAS PLANT SHUT DOWN ON SOUTH EAST MOTOR VIBRATION, ROUTING ITS INLET TO FLARE.
Action taken UPON TROUBLESHOOTING OPERATIONS FOUND THAT THE K-3502 REINJECTION COMPRESSOR HAD SHUT DOWN ON SOUTH EAST MOTOR VIBRATION. OPERATIONS LOADED ALL OTHER REINJECTION COMPRESSORS TO THEIR MAXIMUM ALLOWABLE CAPACITIY IN ORDER TO LIMIT EMISSIONS DURING THIS DOWN TIME. OPERATIONS ATTEMPTED TO PUT THE COMPRESSOR BACK ONLINE BUT THE UNIT SHUT DOWN ON MOTOR FAULT. OPERATIONS CONTACTED FIELD PERSONNEL TO CUT INLET GAS TO THE PLANT IN ORDER TO LIMIT EMISSIONS DURING THIS DOWN TIME. OPERATIONS THEN CONTACTED COMPANY ELECTRICIAN AND COMPANY AUTOMATION TECHNICIAN TO RESPOND TO LOCATION. ONCE ALL REPAIRS ARE MADE THE UNIT WILL BE PUT BACK ONLINE, ELIMINATING THE EMISSION.

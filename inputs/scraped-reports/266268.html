Regulated entity name CELANESE CLEAR LAKE PLANT Physical location 1000 ACRE TRACT IN BAYPORT INDUSTRIAL DISTRICT KM 597Q
Regulated entity RN number RN100227016 City, County PASADENA, HARRIS
Type(s) of air emissions event: EMISSIONS EVENT Event began: 08/27/2017 2:28AM
This is based on the: FINAL REPORT Event ended: 08/27/2017 3:06AM
Cause Two plant boilers tripped during Hurricane Harvey. As the temperature in the unit decreases, more ethylene is absorbed by the carbonate solution and increases in pressure until the the CO2 vent opens to atmosphere.
Action taken Operations responded quickly to rising CO2 levels. Attempted all known mitigation strategies to lower ethylene. When that was unsuccesful, the unit was manually shutdown and brought to a safe state.
Emissions estimation method Monitoring data.

Type(s) of air emissions event: EMISSIONS EVENT Event began: 08/21/2017 11:00PM
This is based on the: FINAL REPORT Event ended: 08/22/2017 6:18AM
Cause The ESD discharge blowdown valve on Unit 737 partially opened and release natural gas to the atmosphere. Investigations into the incident could not definitively determine one exact cause; however, multiple possible causes were evaluated. These potential causes included control power interruption, suspect versa valves, suspect wiring inside conduit and PLC issues.
Action taken Upon discovery, the unit was shut down, the valve was closed, and the unit was reset. Corrective actions included replacing versa valves, upgrading PLC programming, replacing suspect wiring inside conduits, and replacing power system batteries.

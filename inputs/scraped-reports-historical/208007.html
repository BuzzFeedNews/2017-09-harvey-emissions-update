Regulated entity name VALERO PORT ARTHUR REFINERY Physical location 1801 S GULFWAY DR, PORT ARTHUR, TX
Regulated entity RN number RN102584026 City, County PORT ARTHUR, JEFFERSON
Type(s) of air emissions event: EMISSIONS EVENT Event began: 01/02/2015 7:20PM
This is based on the: FINAL REPORT Event ended: 01/02/2015 11:50PM
Cause At Hydrocracking Unit 942 (HCU-942), Recycle Hydrogen Compressor K-2100 tripped due to a drop of lubrication oil pressure, resulting in a high rate depressure to flare and shutdown of HCU942. During the shutdown of HCU-942, hydrocarbons were carried over to and upset the amine treatment system, resulting in excess emissions from Sulfur Recovery Unit 545 (SRU-545). The amine system upset also caused excess emissions from several refinery fuel gas combustion devices due to elevated H2S in refinery fuel gas.
Action taken Refinery personnel responded as quickly as possible to mitigate the flaring by following established procedures. Production was curtailed tominimize impacts. The automatic shutdown of HCU-942 was completed.
Emissions estimation method Flaring emissions were calculated using flare gas flow meters, historical laboratory analysis, and established agency emission factors.

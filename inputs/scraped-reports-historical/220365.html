Type(s) of air emissions event: EMISSIONS EVENT Event began: 09/19/2015 12:30PM
This is based on the: FINAL REPORT Event ended: 09/21/2015 3:43PM
Cause The Motiva Port Arthur Refinery experienced an emissions event due to the discovery of a vapor leak on the Fluid Catalytic Cracking Unit (FCCU) Main Factionator tower. The leak resulted in the FCCU being shutdown and placed on circulation until unit startup.
Action taken Operations stabilized and placed the unit into circulation. Repairs were conducted on leak. Once repairs were completed, the unit was started up and placed back into operation.
Emissions estimation method Engineering Estimates.

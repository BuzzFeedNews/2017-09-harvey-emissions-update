Cause The decomp can be attributed to flashing the first jacket water zone. Inadequate cooling in the reactor allowed for a hot spot which caused a decomp. Action taken Interlocks were tripped stopping reactor feed and catalyst feed to the reactor as designed. The remaining reactor inventory was purged to the flare to minimize emissions.

Regulated entity name TOTAL PETRO CHEMICALS & REFINING USA PORT ARTHUR REFINERY Physical location INTX OF HWY 366 & 32 ST
Regulated entity RN number RN102457520 City, County PORT ARTHUR, JEFFERSON
Type(s) of air emissions event: EMISSIONS EVENT Event began: 01/03/2015 1:27AM
This is based on the: FINAL REPORT Event ended: 01/03/2015 3:27AM
Cause Total Port Arthur Refinery is equipped with a Flare Gas Recovery System (FGRS) that serves to reduce flaring emissions. Due to an upset at Unit 860, the FGRS was oversupplied with process gases, resulting in excess emissions. This event was sudden, unexpected and could not be avoided. A formal investigation is underway with results available, upon request, when completed.NOTE: THIS STEERS 208010 (1/3/15)AND THE PREVIOUS STEERS 208009 (1/2/15) ARE BEING INVESTIGATED AS A CONTIGUOUS EVENT.
Action taken The refinery took actions to reduce steam consuption at unit 860, reducing the steam to the coke drum.
Emissions estimation method Engineering Calculations and AIMS.

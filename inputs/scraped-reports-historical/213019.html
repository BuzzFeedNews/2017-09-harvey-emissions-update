Regulated entity name SWEENY REFINERY Physical location BRAZORIA & MATAGORDA COUNTIES
Regulated entity RN number RN101619179 City, County OLD OCEAN, BRAZORIA
Type(s) of air emissions event: EMISSIONS EVENT Event began: 04/11/2015 9:23AM
This is based on the: FINAL REPORT Event ended: 04/11/2015 12:16PM
Cause Two wires became disconnected inside the Trisen governor controller on the C-100 compressor located at Unit 4. The communication failure due to the wiring failure caused the governor to go to minimum speed and then trip out and shutdown. This resulted in the FCC gases from Unit 3 to be vented to the flare.
Action taken Unit 3 Operations personnel responded to restart the compressor and manually control the speed to return it to its normal mode of operation. Board operators reduced the feed to the process to minimize the flaring.
Emissions estimation method Emissions are based on flare flow meters coupled with online gas chromotograhs, along with H2S CEMS and HRVOC CEMS
